### 🔒 Additional NDA Best Practices

**Safe to Include**:
- ✅ Technical architectures (without internal names)
- ✅ Technologies and tools used
- ✅ Scale metrics (user counts, data volumes)
- ✅ Your specific role and responsibilities
- ✅ Generic industry verticals
- ✅ Public awards and recognition

**Avoid Including**:
- ❌ Client/customer names (unless publicly known)
- ❌ Internal project code names
- ❌ Proprietary architectures or trade secrets
- ❌ Unreleased product details
- ❌ Specific financial data
- ❌ Screenshots of proprietary systems
